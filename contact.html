Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an initial consultation?

+

You can schedule a consultation by calling us at +1 (212) 555-0100, emailing inquiry@pinnacle-wealth.com, or filling out our contact form above. Our Client Services team will get back to you within 24 hours to arrange a convenient time.

What is required for a first meeting?

+

For your initial consultation, please come prepared to discuss your financial situation, goals, and concerns. You may bring any recent financial statements, tax returns, or other relevant documents. Our team will guide you through what information is helpful.

Do you have a minimum asset requirement?

+

Pinnacle Wealth Management serves high-net-worth individuals and families. While we have service minimums, we evaluate each client relationship individually. Please contact us to discuss whether our services are right for your circumstances.

Are your advisors fiduciaries?

+

Yes. Pinnacle Wealth Management is a registered investment adviser with fiduciary obligations to all clients. This means we are legally required to act in your best interest at all times, ahead of our own interests.

How do you charge for services?

+

We offer flexible fee structures to accommodate different client needs. Most of our services are based on assets under management (AUM) with tiered pricing based on account size. We also offer fee-only and project-based arrangements. We'll discuss pricing details during your consultation.

How often will I hear from my advisor?

+

We conduct quarterly performance reviews and are available for ad-hoc communication as needed. Your dedicated relationship manager is available 24/7 for urgent matters. The frequency and method of contact can be customized to your preferences.
