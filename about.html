About Pinnacle Wealth Management

Three Decades of Excellence in Private Wealth Management

Our Story

Pinnacle Wealth Management was founded in 1987 by Edmund Whitmore III, a visionary financial advisor with a singular mission: to provide the nation's most prominent families and institutions with sophisticated, personalized wealth management services grounded in timeless principles of integrity and prudence.

What began as a boutique advisory practice with a handful of clients has evolved into one of the region's most respected private wealth management firms, serving over 500 high-net-worth families and institutional clients. Throughout our 37-year history, we have maintained our commitment to our founding principles: putting clients first, acting with unwavering integrity, and delivering exceptional results through disciplined, thoughtful financial stewardship.

As markets have evolved, economies have transformed, and investment opportunities have proliferated, our team has continuously adapted and refined our approach. Yet our fundamental philosophy remains unchanged: true wealth management is not about chasing returns or market timing‚Äîit's about building and preserving generational wealth through prudent strategy, disciplined execution, and genuine partnership with our clients.

Our Wealth Management Process

1

Initial Discovery

We conduct a comprehensive interview to understand your financial situation, goals, values, and concerns. This discovery process is detailed and thoughtful.

2

Analysis & Planning

Our team analyzes your current situation, identifies opportunities and risks, and develops a comprehensive financial strategy tailored to your needs.

3

Strategy Presentation

We present our recommendations clearly and discuss each element of your personalized wealth management plan with you in detail.

4

Implementation

Once you approve the strategy, we coordinate implementation with your attorneys, accountants, and other advisors as needed.

5

Ongoing Management

We monitor your portfolio and financial plan continuously, making adjustments as markets change and your circumstances evolve.

6

Regular Review

We conduct quarterly reviews to discuss performance, market conditions, and any changes to your financial picture or objectives.
